Hack for Social Good

With the rapid advancement of technology, it is important to use its power in ways that benefit society. It can be so easy to misuse technology at the cost of things like social wellbeing and the natural environment. HackDavis challenges it‚Äôs participants hack for social good, and create an opportunity for us to explore the intersection between technology and society.

Health

Develop a product that encourages people to live a healthier lifestyle. Help predict and prevent illnesses before they affect the body. Provide transparency and analysis of health data for consumers. Make it easier for rural or underdeveloped communities to access healthcare.

Environment

Aggregate public data to analyze or improve government green energy policies. Design solutions to reduce energy consumption in habitable spaces. Monitor the environment with IoT sensing technology. Neutralize energy-wasting practices.

Education

Discover new ways to faciliatate learning within the classroom. Enable accessible education for individuals with learning disabilities. Create visualizations for abstract concepts to develop intuition and reasoning.
